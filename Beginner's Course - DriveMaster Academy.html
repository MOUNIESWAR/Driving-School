Beginners Course

Start your driving journey with our comprehensive beginners course. Learn in our specially equipped Maruti Suzuki Swift, perfect for new learners.

Course Overview

Our comprehensive beginner's course is designed for first-time drivers who want to build a strong foundation in driving. With experienced instructors and a structured curriculum, you'll learn everything from basic vehicle controls to essential driving techniques.

What You'll Learn

Vehicle Basics

Learn about car controls, dashboard indicators, and basic vehicle maintenance.

Driving Fundamentals

Master steering, acceleration, braking, and parking techniques.

Traffic Rules

Understand road signs, signals, and essential traffic regulations.

Safety Practices

Learn defensive driving and emergency handling procedures.

Course Duration

The beginner's course consists of:

  • 20 hours of theoretical classes
  • 30 hours of practical driving sessions
  • 5 hours of simulator training
  • 2 mock tests

Prerequisites

  • Must be at least 16 years old
  • Valid learner's permit
  • Basic understanding of English
  • Good physical and mental health
